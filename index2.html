About Jönköping

Jönköping city is located at Jönköping county nearby the beautiful lake Vättern. The population is around 93,000 habitants with a city with an international atmosphere due to the Jönköping University.

What can you do here?

The city is surrounded by nature with some beatiful hiking places. The lake vättern is also a nice place to spend the summer and to do kayaking. Besides the activities in the nature, the city offers good bowling places, restaurants and the asecs shopping.

What are good places to go out?

  • Jönköping county museum
  • Roselundsbadet
  • Elmia
